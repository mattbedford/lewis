St Peter’s Consort

Identity, Posters & Socials / 2018-2026

Identity, poster design and social media for an established choir.

Since 2018, leading a visual transformation for St. Peter’s Consort, a choir with over four decades of history. Prior to the partnership, the group’s public materials lacked a cohesive brand voice. The goal was to move their image away from ‘homemade’ aesthetics toward a professional, high-impact identity that matches the calibre of their performances.

The core of this evolution was a strategic design system. A disciplined typographic template established a clear information hierarchy, ensuring the choir is instantly recognisable across the region. This framework provides the anchor for their brand while leaving generous space for bespoke imagery that captures the unique spirit of each concert.

Over the last eight years, this shift in visual professionalisation has helped redefine the Consort’s reputation — transitioning them from a small local group into a widely recognised and respected fixture of the regional music scene.
