Kate & Tom

Wedding Identity & Stationery / 2022

For Katie and Tom's wedding, the brief was to blend two distinct passions: Tom's career in the horse racing industry and Katie's love for the 60s and 70s pop culture. The result is a bold, non-traditional identity that trades classic wedding tropes for a "race-day" aesthetic filtered through a technicolor lens.

The typographic direction pays homage to early pop music and the bold, expressive fonts of the late 60s, creating a fun and carefree atmosphere. This was paired with a clever "Race Card" seating plan, where guests were listed as runners on a scorecard and tables were named after iconic grounds — with their home course, York, taking pride of place as the head table.

It's a project that proves bespoke stationery is at its best when it's deeply personal, playful, and a little bit rock and roll.
