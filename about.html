About

A graphic designer based in Halifax, West Yorkshire, with a focus on making things that work as well as they look.

LC

With experience spanning branding, editorial design, and print, I help organisations and independent businesses build visual identities that connect with their audience. Every project starts with understanding the problem — the design follows from there.

I've worked with cycling cafes building their first brand, choirs looking to professionalise their public image, and self-published magazines that aim to rival the quality of national titles. Whether it's a logo, a 140-page publication, or a poster series, I bring the same attention to craft and detail.

Based in Calderdale, I'm drawn to work that has character — brands with a story, publications with a point of view, and clients who care about getting it right.
